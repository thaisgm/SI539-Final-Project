Explore new music!

Ever feel like you're running low on music recommendations? Spending hours scrolling through Spotify and can't find new music to fit your mood? Or do you find new music but don't feel like you know enough about the genre to talk about it with friends?

This application allows you to explore new music through the Spotify API. Also, you can stay up to date with the latest music news!
